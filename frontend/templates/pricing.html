Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll only pay the difference for the remaining time in your billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You can start using Digital Khata immediately after signing up.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. For Business plan customers, we also offer invoiced billing.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
